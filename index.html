ABOUT FITMIND+

What is this website?

FitMind+ is a concept web app that unifies fitness and mental wellness. It provides a daily mood check-in, instant calorie math (Mifflin–St Jeor), and simple AI-style generators for diets and workouts tailored to your inputs — all locally, with no API keys.

How it works

  • Local mood heuristic (no network calls).
  • Calorie estimates via Mifflin–St Jeor + activity factor.
  • Rule-based “AI” diet & workout generators using your inputs.

Try it now

  • Enter your mood and get a supportive, context-aware suggestion.
  • Calculate daily calories, then generate a rough meal structure.
  • Pick equipment & days/week to get a simple workout plan.
